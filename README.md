# Bridge — Real-Time Voice Translation

[![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/python-3.10+-blue.svg)](https://www.python.org)
---

## 🎤 What is Bridge?

**Bridge** is a local, real-time voice-to-voice translation tool that runs entirely on your computer. Speak in English, and hear your words translated to Spanish instantly — with subtitles displayed in real-time.

No internet required. Your privacy is protected because everything runs locally on your machine.
---

## ✨ Features

- 🎙️ **Real-time translation** — Speak and hear translations with ~1.5 second latency
- 📝 **Live subtitles** — See what you're saying as you speak
- 🔊 **Natural Spanish audio** — High-quality voice synthesis
- 🔒 **100% local** — No data leaves your computer
- 💻 **Low VRAM usage** — Runs on ~7GB VRAM (RTX 3070 Ti tested)
- 📱 **Mobile-friendly** — Works on phones and tablets via browser
